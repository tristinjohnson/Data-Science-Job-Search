This section refers to the jobs based off of location. Throughout this page, the states in which this analysis was done will be highighted, along with the low and high end salaries by state, and finally, the low and high end salaries of cities within each state.

*** The salaries below are estimates from Glassdoor.com, based off past employees, and can vary from job to job ***

Job Count in the U.S. by State

The graph below highlights the amount of jobs within each state. This dataset consisted of 10 different U.S. states, as you can see below. California and Texas have the greatest number of jop postings from this dataset, and we will look deeper into all of the states.






Low and High Salary by State

This graph highights the low and high end salaries by state. As seen below, Deleware has the highest salaries within all of their job postings, however, there are only 10 job postings in Deleware, making this information quite unreliable. Looking at California and Texas, we can see there are over 1,000 job postings in both of those states, meaning the salary estimations are more accurate and reliable.
