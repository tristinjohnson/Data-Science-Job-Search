The Salary of a Data Scientist

This section refers to the different aspects of salary when it comes to finding a job. Below, you will find various graphs displaying the differenet salary ranges. Since there are a large amount of different data science titles, we can categorize the job titles into the main subject. This will give you a better idea of the low and high end salaries of several positions.

*** The salaries below are estimates from Glassdoor.com, based off past employees, and can vary from job to job ***

Data Science Job Count by Salary Range

The graph below shows the total amount of jobs within a certain salary range. Looking below, around 53% of the jobs in this datset have a salary of over $100,000, and around 75% of the jobs have a salary of over $75,000.
