Different Types of Data Science Jobs

This section refers to the various types of data science jobs currently on Glassdoor. Throughout this page, you will find the most popular data science job titles, what sectors each job is in, along with the most popular industries of which the jobs are in.

The Most Popular Data Science Job Titles

This graph displays the job titles that are most frequent in the dataset. Looking below, we can see that the titles of "Data Scientist", "Data Engineer" and "Data Analyst" are the most popular job titles within the Data Science field.

The Different Sectors For Data Science Jobs

This pie chart displays the 23 different sectors in which the jobs are within. We can see that Information Technology, Business Services, and Biotech & Pharmaceuticals are the most popular Data Science sectors. This makes sense as these sectors are constantly handling massive amounts of data.

The Top 20 Data Science Industries

This bar chart displays the top 20 industries in the Data Science field. Since there are over 140 different industries, we can simply look at the most popular ones. IT Services, Staffing & Outsourcing and Biotech Pharmaceuticals are the 3 most popular industries.
